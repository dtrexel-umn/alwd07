Restaurants

Walking Distance from Law School

  • Dilla's (Ethiopian): 1813 Riverside Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • Jambo! Kitchen (African): 1939 S 5th St, Minneapolis, MN
  • Malabari Kitchen (South Indian): 414 Cedar Ave S, Minneapolis, MN
  • Afro Deli (African): 720 SE Washington Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • Kimchi Tofu House (Korean): 307 SE Oak St, Minneapolis, MN
  • Bona Vietnamese: 815 SE Washington Ave, Minneapolis, MN

Transportation likely required (but worth it!)

  • 112 Eatery (eclectic), 112 N. 3rd street, Minneapolis, MN
  • Brasa (Creole), 600 E. Hennepin Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • Kyatchi (Japanese), 3758 Nicollet Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • Hola Arepa (Latin American), 3501 Nicollet Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • La Grolla (Italian), 425 Selby Ave, St. Paul, MN. Outside seating on a nice summer evening.
  • Manny's Steakhouse (steak), The Foshay, 9th & Marquette, Minneapolis, MN
  • Capital Grille (steak, seafood, etc), 801 Hennepin Ave, Minneapolis, MN
  • Muffuletta-in-the-Park, 2260 Como Avenue, St. Paul, MN. Outside seating on a nice summer evening.
  • Zelo (Italian), 831 Nicollet Mall, Minneapolis, MN
